The news industry is failing

News outlets have been shutting down. Trust between consumers and news outlets has been in decline. On top of all that, journalists are getting killed for doing their job, and the majority of countries having a "problematic" press freedom situation or worse.

This can leave us feeling hopeless, so we turn to social media for information. Social media, however, was built to show us content that it thinks we agree with, creating an echo-chamber of opinions. Social media have also made it very clear that they have no interest in discerning what is true, fake and malicious.

The solution

News Buff is a purpose built news platform, designed to counteract the bubble affect of echo chambers. News Buff sources content moderators from the community, and has incentive mechanisms built in on every level to encourage unbiased reporting, with punishment for malicious behaviour.
